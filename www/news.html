PyMaemo for Maemo 5 alpha SDK released 2009-04-02¶

  • Updated versions of all packages, now matching the Debian Lenny stable release.
  • Custom packaging was dropped and now we use the standard Debian packages (plus Maemo specific customizations). This means that PyMaemo packages should now be fully compatible with Debian, thus facilitating ports of existing applications from Debian.
  • Dropped python-gpsbt package, since libgpsbt does not exists on fremantle anymore.
  • Added ipython (not installed by python-runtime)
